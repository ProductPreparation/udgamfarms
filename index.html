Udgam FARM & RESORT

TAKE BREAK FROM THE DAILY GRIND & JUMP INTO THE NATURE TO EXPERIENCE ADVENTURE, RELAXATION AND REJUVENATION

COME RELAX & ENJOY

Udgam Farm & Resort is an idyllic escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, located 80 kms north-west of the Patna. Surrounded by lush greenery and clean, fresh air. Our farm and resort offers a peaceful sanctuary from the pollution of urban living. Our guests can indulge in a variety of adventure sports to de-stress and rejuvenate their bodies, while also enjoying the organic fruits and vegetables grown right on our farm. Take a stroll through our orchards and handpick your own produce for a truly authentic farm-to-table experience. For those who enjoy a more rustic way of life, we also offer services for fishing and visiting our poultry farm to pick eggs or chickens. We would be delighted to welcome you to our farm and resort, where we can offer you a truly unique and unforgettable experience.
